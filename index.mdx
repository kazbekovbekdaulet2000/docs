## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your API token">
    Log in and retrieve your API key from your account settings, or call `POST /auth/login` to obtain a Bearer token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first request">
    Pass your token in the `Authorization` header and call any endpoint in the Yume Cloud API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage rentals and sales">
    Create rentals, process payments, track GPS locations, and generate documents — all through the same API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate your tools">
    Connect Yume Cloud to your existing software using the Integrations API.
  </Step>
</Steps>
